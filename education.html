HIGH SCHOOL

I started attending Sekolah Sri Seremban. The school includes three levels which are the preschool, primary, and high school. The primary school named Sekolah SRI Seremban, while the high school's name is SMI Seri Setia Seremban. My school is a religious and private school that uses projectors for daily lessons. In 2020, when I was in Form 4, the school implemented the use of iPads to replace textbooks. I graduated from Form 5 in 2021, and I also took the SPM exam there. It was very nice and hard journey to finished my high school expecially as fisrt batch for new silibus.
